# Privacy Policy

Copy Selected Tab Links does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

The extension reads the URLs of tabs selected in the current Chrome window only when you click the extension action or its context menu item. It writes those URLs to your clipboard using your configured separator.

The extension stores only your local settings through Chrome storage:

- whether to close tabs after copying
- the separator to place between copied URLs

No browsing history, copied URLs, settings, analytics, or diagnostics are transmitted to the developer or any third party.
